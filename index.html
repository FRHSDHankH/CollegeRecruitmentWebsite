Fitness Centers

Rutgers provides extensive recreation facilities, including gyms, pools, outdoor spaces, and unique features like a climbing wall and roller hockey rink.

Rec Centers & Gyms

Each location has its own combination of aquatic, outdoor, and/or fitness amenities. Make an appointment for a free fitness orientation to check them out in person and arrive ready to break a sweat.

Volunteer Work

A wide range of organizations offers community service opportunities, including New Jersey’s largest student-run philanthropic event, supporting causes from children’s health to human rights and homelessness.

Supporting Students

The Division of Student Affairs provides free or low-cost support in health, advocacy, safety, community, culture, and student life to enhance the Rutgers student experience.
